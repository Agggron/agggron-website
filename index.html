Hi, I'm Aaron!

I am a software engineer who loves to explore the unknown and apply this newfound knowledge towards real-world problems. My formal background is in biochemistry, but I have taught myself programming due to an insatiable curiosity to fully understand how things work. No matter what the system is - an online web application, a key signaling protein, or even a novel video game mechanic - I enjoy learning and creating. I'm excited to be a part of this rapidly-growing industry!

I first started programming because I wanted to create video games. And no, not just the next Bejeweled clone (though I've done that too!). We're talking on the scale of a full-fledged RPG, with its own living, breathing world. It was once a childhood dream, and now I am making it a reality!

Game development is my creative outlet for my own software development skills. I love being immersed in the entire process of designing the architecture, programming the mechanics, and building the software. I've had experience both working solo and collaborating with teams of other programmers, artists, and designers. I've participated in game jams, as well as worked on projects for extended periods of time. I have created games in genres ranging from single-player puzzles, to RPGs, to online multiplayer games.
